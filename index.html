Resume

I am all about giving leverage to people with modest resources so that the world stays diverse and exciting.

In late 2019, I decided to change career paths from hardware engineering, and pursue software engineering. In many of my hardware projects I had also written software, primarily for embedded devices. I had encountered a spirit of openness and sharing in the developer community that I had not seen in the hardware community and I only become more impressed as my journey into software progressed. As I transition to interviewing, I intend to find ways to give back.
